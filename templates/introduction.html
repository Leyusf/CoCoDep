Real-time Collaborative Programming Platform
 
In the modern era of connected cyber-society, collaborative working increases the productivity. Therefore, learning/working in collaboration is one of the central objectives of higher education. In different modules, collaboration among students is always encouraged for sharing of knowledge and experience in terms of group assignments, presentations, and project tasks. However, when it comes to programming, mostly the students learn programming through books, teacher’s notes and guidelines, online tutorials, lab manuals, etc.
 
The development of ReCoPP (a web-based application) will provide online platform to experience programming in collaboration. ReCoPP with several features (i.e., group matching, collaboration board, real-time code editing, and audio/text chat, etc.) will assist both students and teachers in collaborative learning environment.
